How does our design service work

Client send design request

By clicking "send design request" button, you will be directed to another web page where you will fill a questionaire describing all the necessary information about the logo design, after filling the questionaire, click "submit" button to sumbit to 99LogoStudio support team.

99LogoStudio will then review the submission.

After client submiting the questionaire, 99LogoStudio support team will review the form and double check if every information is correct and workable, after review, we will send you notification message to your email address notifying whether your request is approved or rejected.

Start to work

After being approved, Our team will start to work on the design project based on different package guildlines and the full information  you have submited, we will regularly send milestone messages to your email address and you can provide feedbacks to ensure that the design will be on the right direction. When design is complete, we will send you 3 slightly different logo designs in low resolution jpeg file, you will have to pick the one you prefer, you can send revision requests based on the package you choosed.

Make payment & receive the design

When you are satisfied with the final design,we will send you payment request to your email, once payment is completed, we will send you all the necessary design files to your email, high resolution transparent png both in RGB and CMYK mode, jpeg, pdf, svg, eps, etc.. and the original "ai" design file.
